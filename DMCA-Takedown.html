DMCA Takedown / Copyright Infringement

Use this form to submit a DMCA takedown notice for copyrighted material on Allowsi. Read the instructions below — incomplete notices may be rejected.

Need help? Contact: muskratbro@proton.me

Submit a Takedown Notice

This form creates a prefilled email in your default email client. It does not send the notice for you. If you prefer, copy the notice text below into your own email and send to the address above.

We will acknowledge receipt and begin our review within 3 business days. If we remove content, we will notify the uploader and provide instructions for a counter-notice.

What we need (required for a valid DMCA notice)

  • Your contact information: name, email, phone (optional)
  • Identify the copyrighted work: title / registration / description
  • Identify where the infringing material is hosted on our site: one or more exact URLs
  • A statement of good faith: "I have a good faith belief..."
  • Signature: typed full name (counts as signature)

Owner handling process (what we do)

  1. We review the notice for completeness within 3 business days.
  2. If valid, we remove or disable access to the content and notify the uploader.
  3. We provide the uploader with the takedown details and instructions for filing a counter-notice.
  4. If a counter-notice is received, we follow statutory steps (may restore content after 10–14 business days unless owner files suit).

Privacy: We will only use the information in your notice to process the claim and notify the uploader. Do not include sensitive personal data beyond what’s required. For legal advice, consult an attorney.
